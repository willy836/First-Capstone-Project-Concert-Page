Featured Performers

Coast Actors

Representatives of Coast Region in Cultural Performances

Coast Actors is one of the most popular and gifted group of men actors in Kenya. The group has won several accolades for producing some of the best cultural plays in the country.

Central drama group

Representatives of Central Region in Cultural Performances

Central drama group is known for its brilliant performances in drama and cultural dances. Since making their debut in 2010, they have gained massive recognition and continues to compete at national level.

Eastern women drama group

Representatives of Eastern Region in Cultural Performances

Eastern women drama group is the total bomb. This group of actresses has featured among the most talented actresses in Kenya for five years in a row. The Eastern women drama group leaves its audience always wanting more because of their scintillating performances.

Nyanza drama starlets

Representatives of Nyanza Region in Cultural Performances

Nyanza drama starlets comprises of young and talented women. This group is best known for its domination in the inter-county drama competitions. Majority of the women in this group are also great dancers and have careers in modelling.

The North drama group

Representatives of Northern Region in Cultural Performances

The North drama group comprises of enthusiastic men and women who began their acting and dancing in 2010. They gained popularity in 2018 and have since become one of the most loved group by the audience for their performances.

Western drama group

Representatives of Western Region in Cultural Performances

Western drama group is made up of talented men and women who made their acting debut in the early 2000s. They have stayed dedicated to cultural performances and they are always among the contenders for the top position in annual cultural festivals.
