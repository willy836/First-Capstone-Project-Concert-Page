THEATRE DRAMA BLISS
ANNUAL FESTIVAL 2022

This event is organized to provide an opportunity for all Kenyans to show their talents to the entire country and also the world. It is also a good platform for actors and actresses who may not be well known to shine and get awesome opportunities to pursue their careers.

This event is organized to provide an opportunity for all Kenyans to show their talents to the entire country and also the world. It is also a good platform for actors and actresses who may not be well known to shine and get awesome opportunities to pursue their careers. At the end of the event, winning performances are awarded with presents. All participants are also given certificates of participation.
To all talented Kenyans who may want to participate and everyone who enjoys cultural performances, please join us and enjoy our diverse culture during this entertaining event. Thank you.
